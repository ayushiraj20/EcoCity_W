Legal & Privacy

Your privacy is
non-negotiable.

EcoCity is built with a privacy-first mindset from the ground up — no compromises.

🔒
Data Minimalism
Only essential data stored

EcoCity stores only what is strictly necessary — your task completion status, Eco Score, and city progress. Nothing more, nothing less.

📱
On-Device First
On-device & iCloud only

Your city's 3D rendering and task progress are handled locally on your iOS device or synced privately via your personal iCloud account.

🚫
Zero Tracking
No third-party trackers

We do not sell your data or use third-party behavioural trackers. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks in the app.

👶
COPPA Compliant
Safe for all ages

EcoCity collects no personal information and is fully compliant with COPPA — safe for use by players of any age, anywhere.
